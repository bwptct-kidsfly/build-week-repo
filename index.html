Traveling with kids made easy.

Making Travel A Breeze

Juggling the responsibilities of children, bags, and the itinerary can be intense. KidsFly looks to provide a concierge service to provide relief to all parents. Therefore we take pride in managing your whole trip and getting you to your flight on time.

KidsFlyConnect

With our KidsFly app, upon drawing near to the airport one of our fully-vetted KidsFlyConnect staff will be alerted of your estimated arrival so that someone will be waiting for you curbside when you arrive.

Pre-Plan Your Trip

KidsFly allows travelers to pre-plan their trip by adding their flight details, luggage type and quantity, as well as a number of passengers and any special needs just by using our simple, intuitive mobile app, so that we can curate the most pleasant check-in and boarding experience possible.
